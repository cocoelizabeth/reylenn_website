about us

Boutique, multi-family development & construction.

Headquartered in the Cedros Design District of Solana Beach, California, Reylenn Properties LLC is a boutique, award-winning, multi-family development and construction company. Our business model is unique because we have an in-house development branch (Reylenn Development) and a construction branch (Reylenn Construction), that acts as a separate company that can be hired by other developers to build their communities.
